# Assignment-2

## Exercise Descriptions

### Exercise 1 – Customer Discount Eligibility
The program asks for a purchase amount and membership status, determines the discount percentage based on business rules, and calculates the final price after the discount.

### Exercise 2 – Employee Performance Bonus
The program asks for annual salary and performance score, determines the bonus percentage, and calculates the bonus dollar amount.

### Exercise 3 – Loan Risk Classification
The program asks for credit score and annual income, then classifies the applicant as Low Risk, Medium Risk, or High Risk.
